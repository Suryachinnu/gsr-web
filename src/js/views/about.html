Our Story: Rising Stronger Through Adversity

GSR Trust works towards building a new compassionate culture, with equality and inclusion as its foundation. We want to empower people and develop new ways to meet challenges in healthcare, education, and employment fields aggravated in the post-pandemic era.
Adversity is a remarkable teacher. The inception of GSR Trust took place when the COVID-19 pandemic hit our world. The pandemic posed enormous challenges and disrupted human life in ways we never imagined.
Within days of the outbreak, - our founders who have earlier cracked many healthcare challenges and worked extensively for social causes - were on the ground working with healthcare professionals, hospitals, and MedTech companies to do what ever it takes to lessen the impact of this adversity. Their extensive ground work helped them gather thoughtful real- time insights in to a crisis that will define an entire generation ahead.
Given the scale and fury of the pandemic and to mount an effective response to it, our founders were actively engaged with other thought leaders, doctors, social groups, educators, professional networks and participated in collaborative sessions for ideas and solutions to emerge.
They, skill fully captured lessons from their discussions and their own first-hand experience against Covid warrior. They gathered insights in areas like healthcare, education, and employability. As a country, we faced some success stories and setbacks; our founders decided to take each breakdown as a challenge and use it as an opportunity to create a breakthrough The result is the GSRTrust.
OUR VISION
At GSR, the trust's vision is to create a sustainable, inclusive, equitable, and compassionate society that thrives and enriches people.
OUR MISSION
Our mission is to drive real social change toward inclusion and equity by focussing on three core areas of healthcare, education, and employment.

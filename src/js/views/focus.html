At GSR, our primary objective is to place people at the center of development. We believe in human dignity, social justice, and solidarity; and are committed to ensuring equitable healthcare facilities, the right to education, and income distribution. We want to empower people with education and employable skills; we want to facilitate access to medical healthcare facilities and protect the rights of children and youth.

Our efforts are to ensure the inclusion of disadvantaged and vulnerable persons and groups in social development. Our three focus areas of Healthcare, Education, and Employment are stepping stones towards this cause.

HEALTHCARE

In healthcare, we want to adopt a holistic strategy. Therefore, our focus area are establishing, developing, maintaining, and running dispensaries, laboratories, maternity homes, child welfare centers, old age homes, orphanages, home for destitue women and differently-abled childern, and providing financial assistance to the disadvantaged or weak for medical treatment.

The trust, in its fold, has veterans from the healthcare filed who have worked at the ground level and are aware of the challenges, the neccessary immediate steps, and long term goals to be in place that we need to address.

EDUCATION

Today's 21st-century education presents unique challenges and opportunities to students. we attempt to start at rail-blazing journey that uplifts education and makes it accessible to everyone. we are in pursuit of a transformed learning experience for our childern.

We also want to focus on new and effective ways of imparting education. For this, we are in touch with Educators, Education institutes, Social workers, Reformers, and change agents. we also propose to establish, develop, maintain, and run educational institutes at all levels, from primary school to professional colleges, Universities for disseminating education and assisting in academic research and development.

EMPLOYABILITY AND UPSKILLING

Our goal is to create a world that is equal for all. Exclusivity to be out for an inclusive society. This starts by bringing in economic and social equality and by creating equal opportunities with self-reliance this is facilitated through Thought-Leaders, Social reformers, Educators, and Experts who can create opportunities, impart skills, identify ways and means to build capacity in people and make them employable.

LETTER

Dear All,
This year is a mixed bag of emotions. We celebrate the Amrut Mahotsav of India's independence while battling a deadly Pandemic, everyday. Our freedom, glorious 75 years of independence, is a hard-won gift given to us by our predecessors. Now we have the opportunity to create shared prosperity at an unprecedented level, especially after the pandemic.

No need to mention the way the pandemic has affected and changed our lives. We have been through it together. Each of us have been affected, witnessed its effect, fought, braved the situation and emerged stronger from it. Now, we want to focus on the opportunity the pandemic has provided us to do something unique, to reach out others, bring a difference, and create an impact. Seizing of this opportunity depends on you, me, and us. We have a chance that may perhaps not come again for another millennium: to build a society that reflects our deepest values. In a world seemingly going in a multi directions at once, we can, and must, choose a movement with focus, and intention. We need to choose our future and we shall.

Despite the unprecedented growth that our country has witnessed, the advances in Medicine and Technology, there are still many of people in our country who do not have access to quality healthcare, a good education, and have Employment that will dramatically affect their lives and future. However, these problems can be surmounted by fervently believing in the cause, by joining our hands, coming together and commit ourselves to the cause.

Formation of GSR Trust is our first step towards bringing in the change we wish to see. It is our tribute to our country and a way of pay back to the society. So join us, as we embark on this committed journey to bring about that change. With your support and solidarity, we will rise stronger through this adversity.
